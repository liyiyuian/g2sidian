G2sidian — Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-06-28

Summary: G2sidian is self-hosted and private. The developer operates no server and collects no
data about you. Your Obsidian notes stay on your own machine.

WHAT G2SIDIAN IS
G2sidian is a smart-glasses app for viewing and voice-capturing your Obsidian notes. A small
backend that you run on your own computer reads your Obsidian vault files directly. The glasses app
reaches this backend only over your own Tailscale network. Access is token-required, and the backend
is bound to loopback and exposed only within your tailnet.

NO CENTRAL SERVER
There is no central or third-party server operated by the developer. Your notes are not uploaded to
the developer, synced to any cloud, or shared with anyone. There are no user accounts and no
analytics, telemetry, or tracking.

WHAT DATA IS PROCESSED AND WHERE
Your vault contents (folders, note text, search results, and Obsidian Tasks and Dataview query
results) are read by your own backend and displayed on your glasses. Notes you capture or append are
written back to files in your own vault by your own backend. This data flows only between the glasses
app and your own backend over your own network.

LOCAL STORAGE
Your backend URL, access token, chosen vault, and preferences are stored only on your own phone.

OPTIONAL VOICE TRANSCRIPTION (THIRD-PARTY DISCLOSURE)
Voice capture is off unless you enable it by configuring an OpenAI Whisper API key on your own
backend. If voice is enabled and you dictate, the recorded AUDIO of your dictation is sent from your
backend to OpenAI for transcription, using your own API key, and the returned text is written into
your note. In that case your dictated audio is processed by OpenAI under OpenAI's own terms and
privacy policy. If you do not configure a Whisper API key, voice transcription is disabled, no audio
is recorded or sent anywhere, and you type your notes on the phone instead — in which case nothing is
sent anywhere except your own backend.

PERMISSIONS
The app requests "network" (to connect to your own backend, and only if voice is enabled, to reach
OpenAI for transcription) and "g2-microphone" (for optional voice capture and append, only when you
start a voice capture).

DATA RETENTION AND DELETION
The developer stores none of your data. Your notes live in your own Obsidian vault on your own
machine; you control them entirely. Any OpenAI API key you provide is stored on your own backend, not
by the developer. Removing your OpenAI key disables all audio transmission; uninstalling the app and
stopping the backend ends all access.

CHILDREN
G2sidian is a personal productivity tool and is not directed at children.

CHANGES
If this policy changes, the updated text will accompany the app listing.

CONTACT
