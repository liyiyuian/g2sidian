APP NAME
--------
G2sidian


SUBTITLE / TAGLINE
------------------
Your Obsidian vault on your glasses — self-hosted, private, hands-free.


SHORT DESCRIPTION
-----------------
View and voice-capture your Obsidian notes on Even G2 glasses, hands-free. A tiny
backend runs on your own computer, reads your vault files directly, and is reachable
only over your own Tailscale network (token-required, loopback-bound). No central
server, no accounts, no analytics — your notes never leave your machine.


DESCRIPTION
-----------
G2sidian puts your Obsidian notes where you can just glance at them. Pick a vault once
on your phone and your glasses open straight into it — no menus to dig through, no phone
in your hand.

What you can do, hands-free, on the glasses:
• Glance: open to a clean, readable view. Your Obsidian Tasks and Dataview (LIST/TASK)
  query blocks render as live results — handy for a quick "what's due today." Common task
  dashboards built with dataviewjs render too; other dataviewjs blocks show a placeholder.
• Browse and read: move through your vault's folder tree and read notes rendered to clean
  lines, with Obsidian markdown flattened (wikilinks, callouts, tasks, tables, headings).
• Search: find a note by full text, filename, or tag.
• Capture by voice: speak a thought into today's daily note (or an inbox) without reaching
  for anything. Reading a note? Speak to append to it. Or just type on the phone instead.

Gestures: tap = open/capture, double-tap = back, swipe = scroll.

Private by design: a small backend runs on YOUR own computer and reads your Obsidian vault
files directly. It is loopback-bound, token-required, and reachable only over your own
Tailscale network. There is no central or third-party server, no accounts, and no
analytics. The developer collects nothing, and your notes never leave your machine.

Voice is optional. It needs an OpenAI Whisper API key on your own backend. If voice is
enabled, the dictated audio is sent to OpenAI for transcription (see the privacy policy).
Without a key, you simply type on the phone and nothing is sent anywhere but your own
backend.

Requires a one-time backend setup on your own computer (your Obsidian vault folder plus
Tailscale; an OpenAI Whisper API key only if you want voice). G2sidian is an independent
app: not affiliated with Obsidian, no desktop Obsidian plugin required, no cloud sync, and
no multi-user server.

PERMISSIONS (justifications)
----------------------------
network — Connects the glasses app to your own self-hosted backend over your private
Tailscale network to read your Obsidian vault, run searches and queries, and save captured
notes. The app talks only to the backend URL you enter in Setup; no other servers are
contacted, except OpenAI for transcription if you enable voice.

g2-microphone — Captures your voice for hands-free quick-capture into a daily note or inbox
and for appending dictation to the note you're reading. Used only when you start a voice
capture; if you leave voice disabled, no microphone audio is used and you type on the phone.


PRIVACY POLICY
--------------
See privacy.txt (host it somewhere linkable). In short: G2sidian is self-hosted; the
developer operates no server and collects nothing. Your notes stay on your own machine and
flow only between the glasses app and your own backend over your own Tailscale network. No
accounts, no analytics. Backend URL/token/vault choice are stored only on your phone. Voice
is optional — only if you enable it (with your own OpenAI key) is dictated audio sent to
OpenAI to transcribe.
