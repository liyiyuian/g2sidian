Gbsidian puts your Obsidian notes where you can glance at them on Even G2 glasses, hands-free.

Setup (self-hosted): clone github.com/liyiyuian/g2sidian and run ./install.sh - it sets up your backend and builds your app. Then pick a vault on your phone; the glasses open straight into it.

- Glance: Obsidian Tasks and Dataview (LIST/TASK) queries (and common dataviewjs task dashboards) show as live results.
- Read: notes flattened to clean lines (wikilinks, callouts, tasks, tables, headings).
- Search: by full text, filename, or tag.
- Voice capture: speak into a daily note or inbox; while reading, speak to append. No key? Type on the phone.

Gestures: tap = open/capture, double-tap = back, swipe = scroll.

Private: the backend runs on YOUR own computer, reads your vault directly, over your Tailscale network (token-required). No central server, no accounts, no analytics. Voice is optional (your own OpenAI key; audio goes to OpenAI to transcribe). Not affiliated with Obsidian.
